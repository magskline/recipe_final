How to Make...

Potato Dumplings

This recipe is incredilby simple and delicious! But be prepared because it is also incredibly messy to make but so much fun!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 cup of cold mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbs of sour cream

That's it!

Step 1:
Combine mashed potatoes into flour (easiest way is to use clean hands to mix) then in a seperate bowl beat together egg and sour cream.

Step 2:
Combine flour and egg mixture into mashed potatoes and flour then let rest for 10 minutes.

Step 3:
Roll out rested dough and cut into coin sized bites, keep in mind the larger the size the longer they will cook.

Step 4:
Boil salted water in large pot and cook dumplings until they float (10min) then saut√© in melted butter and serve.

Tips:

  • For parties larger then 4 quarduple the batch!
  • Can be easily made gluten free with gluten free flour!
